Indian And Exotic Birds

Binomial Name:Gracula religiosa

Kingdom:Animalia

Conservation Status:Least Concern

Recognized Species:G. r. andamanensis Beavan, G. r. batuensis, G. r. halibrecta Oberholser, G. r. intermedia, G. r. palawanensis, G. r. peninsularis, G. r. religiosa, G. r. venerata

Is a resident breeder from Kumaon division in India east through Nepal, Sikkim, Bhutan and Arunachal Pradesh, the lower Himalayas, terai and foothills up to 2000 m ASL

Hill Myna

Binomial Name:Ciconia ciconia

Kingdom:Animalia

Conservation Status:Least Concern

Two recognized subspecies:C. c. ciconia, C. c. asiatica

Is a long-distance migrant, wintering in Africa from tropical Sub-Saharan Africa to as far south as South Africa, or on the Indian subcontinent

White Stork

Binomial Name:Psittacula krameri

Kingdom:Animalia

Conservation Status:Least Concern

Asian subspecies:Indian rose-ringed parakeet (P. k. manillensis), Boreal rose-ringed parakeet (P. k. borealis)

Large Indian Parakeet

Binomial Name:Ardea cinerea

Kingdom:Animalia

Conservation Status:Least Concern

Recognized species:A. c. cinerea, A. c. jouyi, A. c. firasa, A. c. monicae

Is a long-legged predatory wading bird of the heron family, Ardeidae, native throughout temperate Europe and Asia and also parts of Africa
