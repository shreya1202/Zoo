Floral Kingdom

There is an astounding wealth of plant life that graces our grounds.With nearly two million plants between the two parks, our grounds represent truly world-class botanical gardens that have been recognized by the Indian Association of Museums. The varied gardens our horticulture staff and our dedicated garden volunteers care for are not only critical for keeping our animals healthy and content, but are also crucial in educating visitors about biodiversity and the importance of habitat preservation
